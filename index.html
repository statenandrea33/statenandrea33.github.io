GEOG 420

Geography 420 is a community based learning course. In this course we are assigned a local project, my group project is optimizing and visualizing a local schools bus stops.

GEOG 422/510

Geography 422/510 is a Geographic Software Design course. It focuses on the creation and design of Python packages. The packages are then used for geospatial analysis and interactive mapping.

POLS 240

Political science 240 is an introduction course to public administration/ public policy. In POLS 240 we focused on learning about the role of bureaucracy, as well as how to write a proper policy memo.

POLS 311

Political science 311 is a contemporary issues in american public policy. In POLS 311 we focused on looking at public problems and finding the root of the problem and potential solutions, as well as problems facing congressional policymaking.

POLS 441

Politcal science 441 is a public budgeting course. In this course the process of government budgeting, especially federal government budgeting, are explored, like budget reform measures and their effectiveness.
